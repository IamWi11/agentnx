The Problem

VA IT Is Buried in Manual Work

Large-scale IT operations at VA OI&T face compounding inefficiencies that stretch staff thin and delay resolution times.

📞

Tier 1 Help Desk Saturation

Thousands of repetitive service desk tickets — password resets, access requests, VPN issues — consume analyst time better spent on complex incidents. Mean time to resolve stretches hours to days.

🚨

Alert Fatigue in Cybersecurity

Security teams receive hundreds of alerts daily across SIEM, endpoint, and network tools. Triage is manual. False positives erode analyst confidence and let real threats slip through queues.

Data Access Requires Analysts

Business users need reports and dashboards but require data team intervention for every query. Self-service data access is limited, creating bottlenecks and delayed decisions.
